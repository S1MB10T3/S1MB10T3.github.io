New York Film & Music Foundation is a nonprofit accelerator geared toward helping artists find success. NYFMF goal is to help artists tell their stories to a broader audience, and connect them to a community that appreciates the arts.

The Start

Understanding the Brand

When I brought on to work on New York Film Foundation's I hosted a workshop on basic design, design thinking, market research, and brand identity. The goal was to make sure that the team have a good base for when I leave to take over the and run the site. Also do to a lack of any design launge or style guide, graphics and messaging on social media was all over the place.

During the Brand Identity portion of the workshop I went over the what is and makes up a brand. With the team, we came up with a handful of brand attributes to help guide me and the rest of the organization.

Research

Two weeks before the workshop I asked the team to provide websites that they like the visual and funcational aesthetics of from the nonprofit and music/media sectors. During the kickoff meeting I presented a few of these websites and a couple of websites of my own chosing and ran a excirse on what apsects of the website did they like and dislike.
