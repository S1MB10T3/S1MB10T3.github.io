Minimal. Dynamic. Playful.

When discussing the goal of the site, we came to the conclusion that the site should reprentet the future inspired by video games like Mega Man Battle Network, Jet Set Radio and NieR:Automata. Aiming for a clean and simple style that is also playful with animations and color choices.

About

20XX Magaizne is a publication showcasing New York City culture, artists, and events with a focus on native New Yorkers of color. 20XX Magaizne have been posting articles on Medium and released first print issue on Oct. 23rd 2020.

Design


Articles

The featured article will be a simple scroll/drag from the homepage (which displays the title and images from that article) with all other articles being accessible from the article directory via Navi.

The Article Directory displays all articles published on the site with 2wen-t gaining a search feature to query content.

Navigational Assistant - Navi

"2wen-t" Modeled by Jonathan Yuen

To make the site playful and Dynamic we discussed adding navigational assistant instead of a simple navbar or menu. The 20XX Team designed 2wen-t. I added the navigational/UX interactions.

Gallery

The gallery section of the site is for image heavy collections. With art and photography in the center of content.
