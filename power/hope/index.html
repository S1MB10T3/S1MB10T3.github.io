Wendy Brown discuses courageous speaking as a way to seek change or discussion. In a way, the same can be applied to using and promoting open source software. Through my years at NYU I’ve convinced my CS friends to install GNU/Linux and have involved others interested in the prospect of using Linux.

The open source community needs more than just programmers and tinkerers. We need the whole spectrum of humanity. There so much we can accomplish together with more open technology.
